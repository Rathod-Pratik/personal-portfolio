battle ground mobile india

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is a multiplayer online battle royale gamedeveloped and published by Krafton. It is a free-to-play game available on Android and iOS devices. Thegame is a clone of PUBG Mobile, which was banned in India in September 2020. BGMI was launched in Indiaon July 2, 2021

free fire

In Free Fire, up to 50 players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to kill other players. The last player or team standing wins the game. The game features a variety ofmaps, including a tropical island, a desert, and a city. Players can also customize their characterswith different outfits and accessories.
